The Challenge

Why O.R.C.A?

The State Crime Records Bureau (SCRB) manages a large and continuously expanding repository of crime-related data from 1100+ police stations across Karnataka. Current systems rely on static dashboards and manual queries, limiting deep analysis and real-time insights for investigators and officers on the field.

Crime Pattern Discovery Criminal Network Analysis Socio-demographic Insights Behavioral Profiling Proactive Crime Prevention Intelligence

Platform Capabilities

Key Features

Built for investigators, field officers, and crime analysts — O.R.C.A brings enterprise-grade intelligence to public safety.

🤖
Natural Language Chatbot

Query crime databases in English, ಕನ್ನಡ, or हिन्दी using plain conversational language — no SQL, no technical expertise needed.

🎙️
Voice-Enabled Interaction

Hands-free voice command support for field officers who need real-time information without stopping to type.

🧠
Context-Aware Conversations

The AI retains session context, remembers previous queries, and refines its analysis based on the flow of investigation.

📄
PDF Export & Reports

Download full conversation history, analysis summaries, and visual insights as court-ready PDF reports.

🕸️
Criminal Network Visualization

Interactive graph visualizations expose hidden relationships between suspects, locations, and incidents.

🗺️
Crime Hotspot Detection

Real-time geographic heatmaps identify emerging crime clusters and seasonal trends across Karnataka districts.

📈
Predictive Analytics

Early warning system powered by ML models forecasts crime surges so law enforcement can act before incidents escalate.

🔍
Explainable AI & Audit Trails

Every AI-generated insight comes with a clear explanation and full audit trail, ensuring accountability and transparency.

🔐
Role-Based Secure Access

Tiered access control for constables, inspectors, and senior officers — every user sees only what they're authorized to see.
